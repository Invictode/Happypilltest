Our Journey & Roadmap

Charting the course for a healthier future in the Maldives, one initiative at a time.

Phase 1

Launch Lifeline in Greater Malé

Our journey begins by addressing the most pressing need: immediate access to primary care. We will launch our flagship service, Lifeline, establishing a strong operational foundation and building brand trust through our user-friendly mobile app and dedicated call center.

Phase 2

Launch Silver Shield in Greater Malé

Building on our home-care infrastructure, we will introduce Silver Shield. This specialized nursing service will cater to the elderly and patients requiring routine post-operative and chronic care, reinforcing our commitment to comprehensive, accessible healthcare at home.

Phase 3

Launch New Dawn in Greater Malé

With our core services established, we will launch New Dawn, our premium sports medicine and rehabilitation initiative. This captures a niche but vital market, positioning Happy Pills as a versatile healthcare provider for everyone from athletes to families.

Phase 4

Nationwide Expansion

With a proven, multi-faceted model, we will begin our national expansion. This ambitious phase involves adapting our logistics and technology to bring Lifeline, Silver Shield, and New Dawn to atolls and islands across the Maldives, overcoming geographical barriers.
